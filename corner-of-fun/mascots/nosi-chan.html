The Story
On January 26, Nate Sawyers and Jackson James had a contest between themselves: who could draw a representation of Nosiphus as an anime character. Sean Crain did not become aware of the contest until February 1, when the first image of Nosi-Chan appeared, Nate's rendition. Jackson sent in his own a couple minutes later, and they asked Sean to choose between the two. The option chosen was Jackson's revision.
