Recent as of Sunday, February 3, 2019 - Due to the January resurgence of Minecraft's popularity, we here at Nosiphus have been playing modded survival, something we haven't done in a very, very long time. It has been going for long enough that we have considered the Times of Transition to be over, and that a new Third Golden Age has been upon us.

There's something else as well. This idea has been floating around in the back of Sean's head for a few years now, but now, he is seriously contemplating the return of the Nosiphus Server Network to operation. The Nosiphus Server Network was the multi-server vanilla network that brought the summer 2014 international coalition to prominence. It had a playerbase of around 50-60 users across various time zones. We currently have pages under development to see it return.
