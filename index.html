Recent as of Friday, August 24, 2018 - Today is the most special day in our history ever. Today, August 24, 2018, is the tenth anniversary of our founding. I never expected this day to get here, and if you had asked me just a mere six years ago whether anything major would come out of the five years since the relaunch, I could not have possibly said yes. And yet here we are, ten years after the moment I walked into the living room and saw that Verizon/Alltel merger advertisment.

Here we are, with countless brand names, ideas, and product concepts, among these being Blue Cove, Red Phones, HandPaw, Pink Beach, SCT, Nosiphus itself, Olivia Jones, WalStar, Sanders & Higgs, Sanders & Hendricks, SeanNet, SeaNET, Crogan, Saturn, Voyager, Professional, Telkit, NosPack, Love, and the countless others.

I could not have forseen the launching of a Minecraft server that led to Nosiphus having international name recognition, nor could I have forseen the memories that I would have, especially in the last five years. Nosiphus has truly defined who I am for ten years, I can't imagine what things would be like without it.

To everyone who's been involved in Nosiphus, present and past, I extend a thank you for helping to build this organization into what it is now. I especially thank Wyatt Mote and former member Maguire Rodgers, the two guys who contributed the most into shaping this company into what it is now. I couldn't have done it without you two.

Thank you all for making it happen.

- Sean Crain -
