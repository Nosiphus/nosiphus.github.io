Installing OptiFine for 1.7.10 Modpacks

OptiFinder is currently not available for Minecraft 1.7.10, but it will be soon.

1. Click the Open Directory button on the dialog box that appears.

2. Go to this website to download the OptiFine installer. Do NOT place this in the "Open Directory" folder.

3. Load the vanilla Minecraft launcher and install Minecraft 1.7.10.

4. Double-click the OptiFine installer, and then click Extract. Extract the file to the desktop.

5. Move the OptiFine_1.7.10_HD_U_VERSION_MOD.jar file into the "Open Directory" folder.

6. Click Play to reload the modpack with OptiFine installed. If the pack fails to launch after this, restart your computer.
