Tekkit To The Limit
by Sean Crain
Written in April 2015
A parody of Take It To The Limit by Eagles

Looking over for some rubber trees
When the sunlight has faded to moon
I was hoping for some copper that I needed
Once in a blue moon

You know I've always been a miner
(Spend my nights digging down)
And it's too dark to see
(Can't seem to turn around)
But the mines I've seen lately
Keep on digging out and mining out
And digging out the same

So give me a modpack
And don't let me die
And Tekkit to the Limit one more time

You can spend all your stuff on BuildCraft
You can spend all your ores for IC2
If RedPower crashed the server tomorrow
Would you use EE2?

And when you're looking for some diamonds
(Everyone has been there)
And it's getting too cold
(Seems the mine is all but bare)
When there's nothing worth taking
Still you look more, looking more
You keep looking for gold

So give me a modpack
And don't let me die
And Tekkit to the Limit one more time

Tekkit to the Limit
Tekkit to the Limit
Tekkit to the Limit one more time
