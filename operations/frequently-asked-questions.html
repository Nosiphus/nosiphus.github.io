Minecraft

Q: What is Minecraft?
A: Minecraft is a sandbox game where you can build to your heart's content. Read this for more information.

Q: What is Telkit?

A: Telkit is our main Minecraft modpack. It powers the Nosiphus Modded Server.

Q: What is NosPack/are the Nosiphus Required Mods/is the Nosiphus Pack o' Fun?
A: These are older packs that powered previous versions of the Nosiphus Modded Server.

Q: Can I join the Nosiphus Server Network?
A: Absolutely! For the vanilla server, the IP is mc.nosiphus.com. For the modded server, visit the installation instructions page, and use "telkit" for the modpack name.

Q: Where did you get the high definition YogLabs texture pack?
A: They are replicas of the Yogscast's. You can get the original replica, as well as a replica map, from here. We have extended it out into a separate mod called YogMod.
