The Rehabilitation of George W. Bush
Sean Crain - December 27, 2017

As much as I detest Trump, my party's willingness to rehabilitate George W. Bush as normal is absolutely disgusting. He did nothing to prevent 9/11 after information had been given to him and knowingly invaded a country that had nothing to do with the attack. Because of him, a million Iraqis and thousands of servicemen are dead or wounded. HE IS A WAR CRIMINAL.
