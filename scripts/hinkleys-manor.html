Synopsis

In late January 2016, I had been getting into the suspense genre thanks to The Twilight Zone. On February 7, 2016, the day before Wyatt began his six-month hiatus from Nosiphus, I wrote out a short story that would've starred he and Nathan. When Wyatt resigned the following day, I replaced his role with Maguire. I continued to expand the story and it changed from a short story into a full-length script for a potential film. By 2017, Nathan and I were the lead actors, and the characters had proper names.

Nate and Jason both contributed to the story in late 2016, and Nathan wrote some of his own material. My father even added his own section to the story. I later refined the material to fit the story's aesthetic.

We started set construction at the end of 2016. I built the shell and general layout of the building, Wyatt decorated the kitchen, Nate did the bedroom, and Nathan helped me fill in the floors. Maguire also helped us build.
